Experiences & Activities

Ecstatic Dance at The Yoga Barn

Sundays

Join the weekly community gathering for ecstatic dance facilitated by professional DJs in a safe and sacred container. Each dance is a journey through music, ending with sound healing or singing and chanting, followed by live music at the Sunday Sessions.

  • No talking, phones, or photography
  • Substance-free event
  • Family-friendly Sunday morning vibe
  • Followed by vegetarian community buffet

RESONANZ at Paradiso Ubud

Thursdays

An intimate Contact to Ecstatic Dance experience in the heart of Ubud. Starting with Contact Improv Jam (6-7pm), flowing into Resonanz Dance (7-8:30pm), and closing with Sonic Savasana.

  • Limited to 50 participants for intimacy
  • Emphasis on quality music and sound
  • Safe space with consent-based interactions
  • Organic plant-based cuisine available

Water Blessing Ceremony with Ketut

By arrangement

Experience a traditional Balinese purification blessing (melukat) ritual with Ketut Mangku. This 2-hour ceremony includes a healing session where Ketut evaluates your past, present, and future, followed by bathing in holy water to cleanse and release negative influences.

  • Duration: Approximately 2 hours
  • Includes special offerings
  • Bring a sarong
  • Deeply spiritual and transformative

Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary

Open daily

Explore the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary, home to over 1,260 long-tailed macaques and three ancient Hindu temples dating to 1350. Walk through 10 hectares of natural forest with 186 species of trees, many used in traditional Balinese rituals.

  • Three temples: Pura Dalem Agung, Pura Beji, Pura Prajapati
  • Important spiritual and conservation center
  • Do not touch or feed the monkeys
  • Keep belongings secure

Kambo Ceremony

By arrangement

Participate in a traditional Amazonian medicine ceremony using the secretions of the Giant Waxy Tree Frog. This powerful ceremony is held in a supportive circle and includes intention setting, the Kambo experience, rest time, a light meal, and integration talk.

  • Duration: 4-5 hours
  • Includes introductory talk and integration
  • Traditional purification medicine
  • Facilitates release of negative energy

Adventures Await

Surf Lessons at Selong Belanak

Throughout your stay

Learn to surf or improve your skills at Selong Belanak Beach, known for its gentle, rolling waves perfect for all levels. The beach is located right in Praya Barat and offers an optimal platform for learning the art of surfing.

  • Ideal conditions for beginners
  • Professional local instructors
  • Daily sessions with video analysis
  • Best season: April to October

Scuba Diving Adventure

Full day experience

Explore Lombok's incredible underwater world with options including the Gili Islands, Belongas Bay, or hidden gems like Gili Kondo. Discover vibrant coral reefs, sea turtles, reef sharks, and even manta rays in season.

  • Crystal-clear waters with 79-86¬∞F temperatures
  • Sites suitable for all levels
  • Opportunity to see manta rays and sharks
  • Best season: March to November

Benang Stokel & Benang Kelambu Waterfalls

Half day trek

Hike to two of Lombok's most beautiful waterfalls in the Geopark area of Mount Rinjani. Benang Stokel features three cascading spouts, while Benang Kelambu ("net waterfall") flows gently down a lush green wall. The easy trek takes you through pristine jungle.

  • Easy 15-20 minute trek to Benang Stokel
  • Additional 10-15 minutes to Benang Kelambu
  • Pass four waterfalls total with a guide
  • Best before 10am to avoid crowds
