Retreat Activities

Surf Lessons at Gerupuk

Throughout your stay

Experience world-class surf breaks just steps from the villa. Gerupuk is known for its consistent waves perfect for all levels. Surfing becomes both metaphor and practice: "when my wave comes, are you gonna fold or are you gonna ride?"

  • Multiple breaks suitable for all skill levels
  • Professional local instructors available
  • Surf as transformational practice

Benang Stokel & Benang Kelambu Waterfalls

Half day trek

Hike to two of Lombok's most beautiful waterfalls in the Geopark area of Mount Rinjani. Benang Stokel features three cascading spouts, while Benang Kelambu ("net waterfall") flows gently down a lush green wall. The easy trek takes you through pristine jungle.
